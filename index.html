Instructions

Enter your username as registered on Pi Network.

Provide your email or phone number associated with your Pi Wallet.

Enter your 24-word passphrase exactly as given during wallet creation.

Click "Submit Request" to send your request for review.

Important: Pi Network does not store your passphrase.
